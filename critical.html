Critical Financing options

Programs we use to build your funding strategy.

Whether you are looking for unsecured working capital, credit-based programs, home equity solutions, or SBA-backed financing, we help you understand which lane makes sense—and how they can work together.

Revenue Based (No Credit)

Unsecured working capital designed around your business deposits rather than your credit score.

  • • Terms up to roughly 36 months
  • • Monthly, bi-weekly, or weekly payment options
  • • Same-day funding with decisions typically within hours
  • • Minimum monthly business deposits around $20,000
  • • No minimum credit score required
Get Approved

Revenue Based (With Credit)

For stronger-credit businesses that want longer terms and more traditional payment structures.

  • • Terms up to about 36 months
  • • Monthly payment options for easier planning
  • • Lower-rate structures available for qualified files
  • • Minimum monthly business deposits around $20,000
  • • Credit profile starting near the high 500s and above
Get Approved

Equity & Credit Based

Home purchase and cash-out solutions for self-employed borrowers looking to tap equity without extensive tax return requirements.

  • • Purchase, cash-out, and rate/term options
  • • No tax returns required in many scenarios
  • • Typically 12+ months in business
  • • One- to four-unit properties
  • • Fast turnaround and extended terms
  • • Credit starting in the low 600s and up
Get Approved

Credit Based (SBA)

SBA-backed programs for acquisitions, expansion, and longer-term working capital with competitive fixed or variable rates.

  • • Access to a broad network of SBA-approved lenders
  • • Prequalification typically within a few business days
  • • Multi-year terms for a wide range of uses
  • • No prepayment penalties on many structures
  • • Best suited for stronger-credit owners and stable financials
