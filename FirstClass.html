Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We have answers.

What are the minimum requirements to qualify?

Minimum requirements vary by product, but generally, we look for businesses with at least 6 months in operation, $10,000+ in monthly revenue, and a FICO score of 500+. Don't worry, applying is free and will not affect your credit score.

Will checking my options affect my credit score?

No. Our simple application uses a "soft pull" which does not impact your credit score. You can see your funding options with zero risk.

How quickly can I receive my funds?

This is our specialty! For many of our products, like the Merchant Cash Advance, you can receive your funds in as little as 24 hours after approval. Term loans and SBA loans may take slightly longer.

What is the difference between a loan and a cash advance?

A business loan has a fixed repayment amount over a set term. A merchant cash advance (MCA) is a purchase of your future sales. You receive a lump sum upfront and repay it with a small, fixed percentage of your daily sales. This means you pay back more when sales are strong and less when they're slow, which many business owners find flexible.
